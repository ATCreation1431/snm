A SUMMARY

A brief overview of how our fertilizers support sustainable agriculture

As S.N.M Agro-Tech, we strongly believe that the growth of India is intertwined with the growth of rural areas. Farmers are the backbone of rural India and it is our responsibility to support them by offering high-quality agricultural inputs and services.

Since our establishment in 2011 at M.I.D.C Parshioni, Dist. Nagpur, we have been committed to continuous development and R&D to meet the evolving needs of farmers and agricultural industry.

With ISO 9001-2015 certification, Trade Mark, and UK Cert Organic Certification, we ensure the highest standards of quality in our products and services. Our diverse product range includes Bio-Fertilizer and Organic Fertilizer, tailored to cater to the specific needs of farmers for improved yields. We strive to go beyond just providing products by offering customized solutions that address farmers' challenges and enhance their satisfaction. Thank you for trusting S.N.M Agro-Tech as your partner in agricultural success.
